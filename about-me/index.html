About Me

Info

hi. i’m ali. sometimes i write things. sometimes those things are useful. sometimes they’re just me trying to figure stuff out while pretending i’ve got it together.

this blog? it’s not a portfolio. it’s not a personal brand. it’s more like a notebook I built just to avoid forgetting stuff and to look cool (joking) — you’re welcome to read it, scribble in the margins, or walk away confused. all are valid responses.

what’s in here, probably

  • Learning new techniques about optimizations
  • Maybe trying learning new stuff about computer graphics
  • Learning math from time to time
  • half-finished projects that might one day become whole (no promises)

Why I Bother

writing helps me untangle my brain. sharing it? that’s the weird part where I hope someone else goes “oh, me too” or “wait, that’s not how i saw it” — and then we both learn something.

I also feel glad when someone writes something that helped me

when i’m not typing into the void

  • staring at a coffee cup wondering where the day went
  • reorganizing my desktop folders (again)
  • listening to the same 3 songs on repeat while pretending to be productive
  • accidentally starting side projects that should’ve stayed as shower thoughts

if any of this resonates — or even if it doesn’t — feel free to say hi: [ali.elmorsy@gmail.com]

no fancy pitch. no newsletter pop-up. just a human on the other side, probably also procrastinating.

glad you wandered by.
